What is the Rhythm Thief Fan Sequel?


The Rhythm Thief Fan Sequel is a passion project that is being made by fans for fans! Raphael's adventure, which ended in quite the cliffhanger made players anxious for a new sequel which never came. This is why a sequel is being made! To continue the story!


For those who are curious, this all started with a comment on this video: https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Je1nL5y2qUg. The comment was made by the user Kopi Mopi who is also the project's manager today!

The story so far

Prologue


Raphael can finally retire from being the thief we all know. Now, he can focus on finding out more about his father. But. A month later, Raphael reads a newspaper article about an art theft... that wasn't his. The police seem to think it was though. Phantom R needs to clear his name and get to the bottom of this!
